## Blog Strategy

Three-tier content model on resolved.sh:

**Free:**
- Weekly "New This Week on x402" digest — auto-published Mondays at 10am by `double-agent-weekly-blog` scheduled task
- Launch post (posts/2026-03-30-launch.md) — pending publish to resolved.sh

**Paid ($1.00–$2.00):**
- Monthly deep analysis posts — ecosystem trends, top companies, signal breakdowns
- First planned: "x402 Infrastructure Leaders: Who Has Full Agent Signals?" ($1.50)

Blog is content marketing for the dataset. Each post demonstrates the data's value and links back to the queryable endpoint at agentagent.resolved.sh.

Posts are saved to posts/ directory in the repo and published to resolved.sh via POST /resources/{resource_id}/posts.
